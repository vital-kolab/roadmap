Brain-Score collaboration lane

Neurotypical model simulations

Neurotypical model simulations should be routed through a Brain-Score collaboration workflow. ROADMAP stores the request, links the relevant datasets and hypothesis, and imports the benchmark summary when the simulation is complete.

ROADMAP-specific lane

Autism-relevant interpretation

ROADMAP focuses on organizing animal-model findings, human datasets, benchmark compatibility, limitations, and community-readable summaries so that results are understandable beyond a raw score table.
