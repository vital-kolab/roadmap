Clinician portal

Review evidence summaries for clinical research and translation planning.

This portal is for clinicians and clinical researchers who want to understand what a model or dataset suggests, how confident the evidence is, and what would be needed before a finding could inform a clinical study, trial design, or product-facing use.

Evidence review

Each ROADMAP evidence card answers the same core questions.

QuestionClinician-facing answer
What was measured?Task, behavior, neural signal, clinical measure, dataset, or model output.
What does it suggest?The specific hypothesis supported by the evidence, with uncertainty clearly stated.
How close is it to clinical use?Translation-readiness level: exploratory, research-ready, trial-planning, or not appropriate for clinical use.
What should happen next?Recommended validation step, dataset need, participant group, or clinical research design.
