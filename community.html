What you can ask

Community questions become part of the research translation record.

Questions help identify where a scientific claim needs a clearer explanation, more evidence, or a stronger statement of limits.

1
What does this research mean?

Ask for a plain-language explanation of a neural hypothesis, model simulation, task, dataset, or reported finding.

2
Who could this help?

Ask how a finding might connect to clinical research, supports, education, or product development.

3
What evidence is missing?

Ask whether a claim has been tested in humans, animal models, clinical settings, simulations, or only one dataset.

What ROADMAP will do with feedback

Community feedback will be used to improve explanations, identify research priorities, revise unclear language, and flag claims that need more evidence before they are communicated outside the research setting.
